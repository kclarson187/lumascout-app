FAQ

Questions, answered.

Still curious? Reach us at support@lumascout.app.

Is LumaScout available on iPhone and Android?

Yes. LumaScout is built with Expo and ships natively on iOS and Android. A web companion is in private beta.

Do I have to share my spots publicly?

Never. Every spot you save defaults to Private. You opt-in to Followers, Public, or Premium on a per-spot basis.

How does Scout AI build itineraries?

Scout AI uses real spots from the LumaScout community, your saved list, and creators you follow. It accounts for sun position, drive time, and your stated shoot type.

Can I sell location packs as a creator?

Yes. Creators on the Pro and Studio plans can list curated packs in the Marketplace and earn from purchases.

What does “Premium” on a spot mean?

Premium spots are paid drops from verified creators. Subscribers see them; non-subscribers see a teaser pin.
